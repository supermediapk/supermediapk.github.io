Our History

Super Media was founded in 2005 by Muhammad Aamir Munir, who served as the CEO, with a vision to provide reliable cable services to the entire city of Jaranwala. Thanks to its exceptional service and customer trust, the company quickly established a strong reputation. Over time, it expanded its reach, extending services to nearby villages and underserved areas, significantly improving the living standards of its customers. In 2019, Super Media broadened its offerings by introducing high-quality, affordable internet services, further solidifying its position as a trusted provider in the region.

From its inception, Super Media has been the leading cable service provider in the region, setting the standard for quality and reliability. Pioneering the introduction of internet services to the local community, it became the first company to bring this essential service to the area. With a customer base exceeding 10,000, Super Media has earned a reputation for excellence, consistently delivering top-tier services and maintaining a strong connection with its growing audience.

Super Media continues to set the standard for cable and internet services in the region. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains at the core of everything we do, and we look forward to further enhancing the lives of those we serve.
